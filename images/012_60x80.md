# Waterfall of Silence

The center is occupied by an almost white vertical mass, like a waterfall, glacier, or stream of light. Blue and gray textures gather along the sides as rock, water, and frozen layers. The composition feels still, yet it contains hidden motion: elongated pale forms descend like drops, traces, or voices.

The work is about the cleansing force of silence. The flow does not roar; it illuminates the space from within. Its whiteness is not empty but made of many small forms, like experience simplified into clarity. The image speaks of passing through cold toward inner lucidity.

## Visual Anchors

- Central white vertical flow.
- Blue textured edges.
- Rhythm of elongated droplet forms.
- Sense of an icy cave.

## Key Meanings

- Purification and renewal.
- Silence as active force.
- Light falling through cold.
- Passage from density to clarity.
