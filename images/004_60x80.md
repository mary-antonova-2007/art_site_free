# Blue Garden of Shadow

The surface is a dense nocturnal garden made of petals, ice, shadows, and mineral cuts. Floral forms appear and disappear inside blue layers, as though the image were seen through cold water or night air. Red accents pulse quietly inside the blue field, giving the composition an inner temperature.

The work suggests a hidden life that does not need daylight to exist. Its flowers are not decorative; they are signs of survival in depth. The image speaks about sensitivity that grows in shadow and about beauty that becomes stronger when it is not immediately visible.

## Visual Anchors

- Layered blue floral masses.
- Red accents inside the cold field.
- Dense overlapping textures.
- A shadowy garden-like atmosphere.

## Key Meanings

- Hidden growth.
- Beauty in nocturnal depth.
- Sensitivity protected by shadow.
- Life continuing beneath cold surfaces.
