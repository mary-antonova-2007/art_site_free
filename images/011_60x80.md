# Solar Whirlpool

A whirling structure is assembled from many small petal-like or scale-like pieces turning around a pale center. A yellow arc resembles a solar wave entering blue water. The whole surface breathes with motion, as if particles were moving in a current, flock, tide, or stream of air.

The painting speaks of the gentle force of circulation. There is no violent collision here: light and water enter each other and create a living rhythm. It is an image of the world as flow, where each small particle participates in a larger return to center.

## Visual Anchors

- Large spiral structure.
- Many small petal-like elements.
- Yellow solar arc.
- Blue aquatic environment.

## Key Meanings

- Unity made of multiplicity.
- Water as moving consciousness.
- Light dissolved in flow.
- Return to center without violence.
