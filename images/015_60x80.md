# Sphinx of Blue Crystal

A stone female figure stands at the center, like an ancient sphinx or a fragment of sculpture. Around her, a blue crystalline environment erupts into shards, ice, minerals, and watery planes that spread like wings. Her face turns upward, as if listening to the sky or remembering its origin.

The work joins archaeology and element. The figure feels ancient but not dead: the blue crystals around her act as an energy of awakening. The image speaks of the feminine figure as a keeper of mystery, one that does not remain locked in the past but opens through the cold light of the future.

## Visual Anchors

- Central stone figure.
- Blue crystalline wings.
- Explosive movement around the body.
- Balance between stillness and energy.

## Key Meanings

- Ancient memory of the feminine image.
- Stone awakening into light.
- Mystery opening through clarity.
- Beauty as the power to outlive time.
