# Crystalline Animal

A figure resembling an animal appears within a blue field, assembled from triangular crystalline planes. It sits at the center yet does not detach from the environment; the geometric structure seems to grow from the surrounding dark matter. The ground suggests night forest, ice cave, or underwater relief.

The work explores the border between the living and the structural. The animal has no soft body; it is made of facets, directions, and reflections. It becomes an instinct that has taken the form of thought, or a thought that has preserved its wildness.

## Visual Anchors

- Central geometric figure.
- White lines assembling the form.
- Dark blue environment.
- Fusion between figure and ground.

## Key Meanings

- Instinct transformed into architecture.
- A hidden being of the night.
- Form emerging from chaos.
- Cold kinetic energy.
