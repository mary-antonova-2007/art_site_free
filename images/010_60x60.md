# Spiral of Water and Sun

The composition turns around a large spiral where blue aquatic segments move gradually toward a yellow, white, and red center. The form recalls a shell, an eye, a planetary orbit, or a diagram of inner breathing. Its order is strict but alive, softened by textures of water and ice.

The deeper meaning is cyclical return. The blue perimeter feels like an endless sea, while the warm center becomes a seed of sun or consciousness. The image suggests that circular movement does not merely repeat; it leads inward toward source.

## Visual Anchors

- Large concentric spiral.
- Transition from blue edge to warm center.
- Segment-based structure.
- Marine and icy textures inside geometry.

## Key Meanings

- Cyclical time.
- Water carrying light.
- The center as inner warmth.
- Cosmos in the form of a shell.
