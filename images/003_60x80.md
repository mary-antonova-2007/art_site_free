# Tree Among Ice

A golden tree rises inside a dense blue crystalline field. Its trunk and branches are warm, dry, metallic, almost ritualistic, while the surrounding space feels cold, mineral, and resistant. The tree does not merge with the environment; it insists on its own vertical flame.

The work is about endurance in an inhospitable world. The tree survives not because the space is gentle, but because its inner heat is stronger than the surrounding ice. It becomes a personal axis: a sign that growth can be an act of resistance.

## Visual Anchors

- Central golden trunk.
- Blue crystalline masses around the tree.
- Upward and outward branch movement.
- Tension between warm life and cold ground.

## Key Meanings

- Vitality in a hostile environment.
- Growth as resistance.
- Fire hidden inside ice.
- The tree as a private axis of survival.
