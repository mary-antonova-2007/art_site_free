# White Flower of Ice

The vertical composition unfolds as a vast white flower rising from a cold blue depth. Its petals seem both soft and mineral, carrying cloud, ice, and stone dust at once. The lower area is darker and denser, as if the flower were emerging from deep water or a mountain fissure.

The work is tied to purification and quiet rebirth. The white form does not attack the space; it opens slowly. It holds the feeling of breath after a long cold season: not victory over winter, but the ability to find tenderness within it.

## Visual Anchors

- Large pale flower mass.
- Cold blue surrounding field.
- Mineral textures in the petals.
- Upward movement from depth.

## Key Meanings

- Purification through cold.
- Tenderness as formal strength.
- Rebirth without noise.
- White light inside blue depth.
