# Stone Split by Light

A gray stone lies on a dark field, crossed by a vertical golden-white crack. Above it, black fragments suggest that the surface has already begun to break. The central cut resembles lightning, root, flame, or a path that light opens through dense matter.

The work speaks of transformation through fracture. Stone stands for closure, weight, memory, and resistance; light divides it not destructively, but as release. It is the moment when inner pressure becomes revelation and the impossible opens from within.

## Visual Anchors

- One large stone.
- Vertical luminous crack.
- Black surrounding field.
- Sharp upper fragments.

## Key Meanings

- Fracture as liberation.
- Light inside heavy matter.
- The overcoming of closure.
- A path born through obstacle.
