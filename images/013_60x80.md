# Fire Figure in Blue Water

A golden-orange figure radiates inside a dense field of blue water. The body appears mythic rather than anatomical, built from flame, shell, and ceremonial fragments. Around it, the blue space both contains and magnifies the heat, turning the figure into a living ember inside depth.

The image speaks of contradiction held without collapse. Fire does not extinguish in water; water does not silence fire. Instead, the two elements create an inner rite of transformation, where passion gains form and depth gains warmth.

## Visual Anchors

- Central warm figure.
- Strong blue surrounding field.
- Radiating orange and gold fragments.
- Elemental contrast between fire and water.

## Key Meanings

- Fire surviving inside depth.
- Passion contained by consciousness.
- Mythic body as energy field.
- Transformation through elemental tension.
